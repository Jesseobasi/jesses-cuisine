Our Policies

Payment Policy

All cart orders must be paid in full to be confirmed. For catering, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your date, with the final balance due 24 hours before your event.

Order Lead Time

All standard cart orders must be placed at least 48 hours in advance. All catering orders must be finalized at least one week (7 days) in advance.

Cancellations & Refunds

We can offer 50% refunds on paid cart orders 24 hours in advance. For catering, cancellations must be made 72 hours in advance; the 50% deposit remains non-refundable.

Pickup Policy

Orders are available for pickup in Baltimore, Maryland. Delivery is available for a fee.

Allergy Disclaimer

Our food is prepared in a kitchen that processes milk, eggs, wheat, peanuts, tree nuts, and shellfish. We cannot guarantee zero cross-contamination. Please contact for more information.

Quality & Satisfaction

We pride ourselves on making your food fresh for your pickup time. If you are unsatisfied with your order for any reason, please contact us immediately so we can make it right.
